Current State: Florida

Elected Officials

Governor
Ron DeSantis

  • The supreme executive power

  • Commissions all state and county officers

  • Does all essential business with the officers of government

  • Responsible for ensuring that laws are faithfully carried out

Lieutenant Governor
Jeanette M. Nuñez

In the event of the governor's death, resignation, or removal from office through impeachment, or in the event of the governor's absence from the state or disability, serves as the acting governor.

Secretary of State
Cord Byrd

  • Chief Election Officer

  • Chief Arts and Culture Officer

  • Head of the Department of State

Auditor General
Sherrill F. Norman

Provides information that is objective, timely, and relevant that the Florida Legislature, the people, public entity management, and other stakeholders may utilize to advance governance and accountability in government.

Financial Chief Officer
Jimmy Patronis

Responsible for:

  • Overseeing the state's finances

  • Collecting revenue

  • Paying state bills

  • Auditing state agencies

  • Regulating cemeteries and funerals

  • Handling fires and arsons

Attorney General
Ashley Moody

Responsible for:

  • Protecting their constituents in Medicaid fraud cases

  • Defending the state in civil litigation cases

  • Representing the people of Florida when criminals appeal their convictions in state and federal courts

  • Protecting Florida consumers from various types of fraud and enforcing the state's antitrust laws

Florida Speaker of the House
Paul Renner

Constitutional officer who serves a two-year term as the head of the House, oversees its operations, and preside over its sessions. They are responsible for choosing committee members and chairs

Florida President of the Senate
Kathleen C. Passidomo

Constitutional officer who leads the Florida Senate for a two-year term; manages the operations of the Senate and presides over its sessions.

Duties include:

  • Selecting a Majority Leader

  • Appointing Senators to standing Senate committees

  • Appointing committee chairs

  • Appointing citizens to a variety of boards and commissions
