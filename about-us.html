About Us - sci_chicks

Jennifer Martinez

"I am currently a high school senior in School for Advanced Studies North who will be attending Washington University in St. Louis during Fall 2023. I want to major in Business and Computer Science and minor in Finance. I am in love with technology and I want to work at or create a company that aids the low-income families of the United States and possibly other countries too.

I was born in the Dominican Republic and immigrated to the United States when I was 7 years old. Through my mom, I felt how hard it was to come to the U.S.A knowing close to nothing and having to study day and night to become a citizen for the goal of being able to vote and gaining more government support."

Alianis Lopez

"I am a high school senior at School for Advanced Studies (SAS) North. My goal is to get a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and become a Software Engineer. Programming is my passion, and I hope to further pursue it in the future.

I was born and raised in Cuba. Being an immigrant myself, I know firsthand how difficult such a transition can be. I’m fortunate enough to have been given a free education when I migrated to the U.S. However, not everyone has such an opportunity to learn more about the U.S. and its history with citizen rights. My hope is that through this website, we’ll be able to better inform immigrants, especially those who aren’t native English-speakers, on their rights and liberties while in the U.S."

Rhode Sanchez

"Hi I’m Rhode and I am a junior at SAS north along with all of my gorgeous team members. I want to attend university and major in either computer science or civil engineering. I aspire to always help my community and better society through the use of technology.

I was born here in the United States but my mother came here from Argentina and my father came here from Cuba. They both had to study and take the citizenship test for the goal of being able to vote and being a true citizen of the United States. They came here not knowing anything about the legal systems in the United States and didn’t know their rights. This is why we created this website for people who are curious and don’t know what rights they hold in this country."

Laura Castellon

"I was born in Cuba but grew up in the Miami community. I am currently enrolled at School for Advanced Studies North where I am a full-time dual enrollment student, getting an AA degree at Miami-Dade College while getting my high school diploma. I am planning on double majoring in Political Science and Psychology. I want to make a change to better my community and think the best way to do it is through a legal career. I aspire to be a civil rights lawyer to be able to fight for people’s rights.

As an immigrant it was difficult transitioning from the culture here and the one in Cuba. I was lucky to go into school right away and be in an environment where I was taught everything I needed to be successful. My parents on the other hand didn’t know English. I remember helping them study for their citizenship test everyday after school. For this reason, I am very passionate about helping others learn about their government."
